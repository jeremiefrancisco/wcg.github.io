VANCOUVER ISLAND

NEAR QUALICUM BEACH

Relax and unwind at Summer Day Beach House, a beautiful new air-conditioned luxury retreat with your own gourmet kitchen. Enjoy more amenities than most 5 star hotels. It’s all here, whether you are a family with children, or enjoy a romantic couples’ getaway or a retreat from the city.

Enjoy Qualicum Bay scallops, Fanny Bay oysters, and local prawns, crab, salmon and halibut prepared in a gorgeous kitchen with quartz counter tops, stainless steel range and dishwasher, and Jenn-Air refrigerator. Dining for six inside at an antique dining table or outside through French doors leading onto a large terrace with a Weber BBQ, gas fire table, comfortable patio set with a peek-a-boo ocean view.

Summer Day Beach House is fully equipped and beautifully furnished. Dim the lights and relax in front of a large Panasonic HD TV and the fireplace, or listen to your favourite music throughout the house on the built-in Russound audio system. Enjoy soft towels and a luxurious walk-in shower with two shower heads in one bathroom, or a relaxing bathtub in the second bathroom. Both bathrooms have granite counter tops and heated marble floors. Curl up in one of three bedrooms (two downstairs, one upstairs) on high quality linens.
